Student Timetabling System

Welcome to your timetable system (by Mark Bennison).

View Timetable

Staff and students alike can view their timetables here. Just login and click the timetable link.

Teaching Staff

Teaching staff can view users, modules, rooms and see when and where any classes are taking place. They also have access to class lists for all classes.

Administration Team

Administrators can edit or add new users, modules, class lists, and rooms. They also have control over timetables by booking of classes in rooms. Only administrators can view the user-roles.
